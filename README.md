# 📊 Financial Order Dashboard

## Overview

The Financial Order Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence dashboard designed to monitor and analyze sales performance, customer orders, revenue trends, product performance, payment behavior, and operational metrics.

This dashboard provides decision-makers with a comprehensive view of financial and order-related KPIs, helping identify growth opportunities, optimize product strategies, and improve overall business performance.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights critical business metrics:

- **Total Revenue:** $1.26M
- **Total Orders:** 1.20K
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 3.54K
- **Average Order Value:** $1.05K
- **Cancellation Rate:** 20.8%
- **Delivery Rate:** 19.3%
- **Return Rate:** 20.6%
- **Average Items per Cart:** 5.5

### Revenue by Product
Analyze revenue generated by individual products and identify top-performing categories.

### Orders by Referral Source
Evaluate marketing and acquisition channels including Instagram, Email, Google, Facebook, and Referral.

### Payment Method Analysis
Understand customer payment preferences across Online Payment, Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card, and Gift Card transactions.

### Order Status Breakdown
Monitor Delivered, Shipped, Pending, Returned, and Cancelled orders to assess fulfillment performance.

### Monthly Revenue & Orders Trend
Track revenue growth and order volume trends over time to support strategic decision-making.

## Business Insights

This dashboard helps answer key business questions:

- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which marketing channels drive the highest number of orders?
- What payment methods are preferred by customers?
- How are cancellations and returns affecting performance?
- What are the monthly revenue and order trends?
- How efficiently are orders being fulfilled?
